MARS

Mars

The Red Planet, or one of our neighbour planets

Everyone has at least heard once from the planet, and most people know quite a lot about it. Also scientists are really interested in finding out more of it, often with really expensive gear. Nasa even sent and operated 3 entire Rovers on there.

Solar System

Mars is the Planet next to earth in the solar System. It is 244 million kilometers away from the sun, and with that farther away than earth.

Its distance to earth is 273 miollion kilometers, on its closest.

Mars exploaration

Nasa, and a lot of other company founded organisations are already putting satelites and even roboters on the surface. Billions of dollars have been put into the research of the planet. Currently, there are some private companies that share intrest in the Planet.

Colonizing Mars

This is something, a lot of people fight about. Should we colonize Mars?? or should we just stay on earth?

A lot of private companies are actually really interested in colonizing Mars, since it could be a good way to expand human life, and maybe even get a lot of profit from it, when it works out.
