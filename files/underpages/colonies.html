Colonizing Mars

A lot of companies and government agencies are both really interested in colonizig the red planet.

How are we gonna colonize Mars?

This is a good qeuestion, and the answer to it is complicated.

Currently, a lot of private companies and government Organisations like NASA, ESA, and some others are interested in colonozing our neightbour planet.

There are a lot of currently active science projects that help us understand living on the Mars better. There are also a couple of projects that help us understand how to live in Space with no gravity.

ISS

The International Space Station (ISS), helps us to do sience in no gravity, and got us a lot of expierence from building structurs, that are filled with air in Space. It also helps us to see the impact of the different enviroment on humans.

The Mars rovers help us understand the Mars better and better. They provide important data from the surface that helps us build fixed stations on Mars.
