Colonizing Mars

A lot of companies and government agencies are both really interested in colonizig the red planet.

How are we gonna colonize Mars?

This is a good question, and the answer to it is complicated.

Currently, a lot of private companies and government Organisations like NASA, ESA, and some others are interested in colonizing our neighbor planet.

There are a lot of currently active science projects that help us understand living on the Mars better. There are also a couple of projects that help us understand how to live in Space with no gravity.

Projects

Listed here are a couple of project that help us explore the living away from Earth:

Satellites

Satellites help us photograph, and use scientific instruments to gather data from Space. They are often used to discover unknown areas of space, and help to understand the universe. But they are also really important for colonizing mars

The are used to gather Data from the planet, but also have a important role in sending data to earth, since sending it over a satellite does not require as much Energy and sending it from the surface of mars. When Humans will land on mars, one important thing is that they have a stable and fast connection back to earth. Since even people living on mars are going to go on the internet, or send valuable research data to ground.

ISS

The International Space Station (ISS), helps us to do sience in no gravity, and got us a lot of experience from building structures, that are filled with air in Space. It also helps us to see the impact of the different enviroment on humans.

On the ISS, we grow plants successfully for multiple years now. Also there are a lot of experiments running, that help us survive in space, without the help of earth. This is just starting currently, but we have learned a lot of new things due to the ISS.

Mars Rovers

The Mars rovers help us understand the Mars better and better. They provide important data from the surface that helps us build fixed stations on Mars.
